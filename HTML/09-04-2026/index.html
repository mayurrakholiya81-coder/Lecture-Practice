Nobita

Shizuka Minamoto is the main female protagonist in Doraemon and the childhood friend/future wife of Nobita Nobi. She is known for her kind, intelligent nature, acting as the moral center, while frequently bathing or being interrupted by Nobita's misuse of Doraemon's gadgets. Their relationship centers on innocent, enduring love and mutual support.
