Nobita Nobi (野比 のび太のび のびた Nobi Nobita), known simply as Nobi or Noby (a possible diminutive of his Japanese name) in the American and UK English versions, is the main protagonist of the Doraemon franchise. He was a failure of a person until Doraemon came from the 22nd century to aid him so he could have a better future in life.
